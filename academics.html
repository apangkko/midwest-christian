Our Academics

MCA’s student-centered approach offers a variety of courses to challenge and engage students, as well as prepare them for college and beyond.

Athletics Spiritual Growth

In addition to our standard-based rigorous academics, our private Christian academy also provides students with opportunities to network with members of their local community and sport industry. Christ and character are at the core of our curriculum.

   Personalized education plans for each student engages students where they are while understanding their dynamic strengths, needs, learning style, and interests. MCA students do not only discover their God-given gifts and purpose, but also the chance to utilize them in service to others for His glory.

 

Imagine Learning

Imagine Edgenuity, formerly Education2020 (E2020), is a standard-based online learning resource for school districts produced by Imagine Learning, which teaches kindergarten through 12th-grade in core, elective, credit recovery, technical, and career subjects, through both remedial and accelerated work.

Imagine Edgenuity is a virtual alternative, and uses pre-recorded videos and primarily focuses on struggling students, and sticking at pace. Along with pre-recorded lecture, Imagine Edgenuity uses interactives and real-world problems.

A dynamic student experience and adaptable content support unique learning journeys. Serve diverse student groups, including National Collegiate Athletic Association (NCAA) student athletes with NCAA-approved courses
