About Midwest Christian Academy

MCA provides a safe environment where students can maximize their abilities and knowledge in Christ. We strive to create a learning and living experience that equips students for a lifetime of success. Our staff is dedicated to ensuring students thrive and reach their goals. We provide a variety of academic and extracurricular activities designed to maximize student achievement. 

Midwest Christian Academy will glorify God by partnering with families to educate, inspire, and equip students to know and serve Jesus Christ in their communities by the power of the Holy Spirit.
