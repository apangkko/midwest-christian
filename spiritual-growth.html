We unashamedly believe in the divine inspiration, entire trustworthiness, and authority of the Bible. Our students, faculty, and staff read, study, and constantly grow in their understanding of the scriptures. These texts serve as the foundation on the way we engage every aspect of life.

School chaplain; devotions, church

"Go therefore and make disciples of all the nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit, and teaching them to obey everything I have commanded you ... " - Matthew 28:19

We to make disciples of Jesus Christ through a biblical school environment that prepares students to glorify God academically, socially, and spiritually.
 

Young adults and especially young athletes are bombarded with a society and culture designed to rob them of their gifts. MCA seeks to wrap its family in the armor of God. Our primary goal is to spread the love of Jesus while creating men of honor and integrity.

Our social standard is the very highest - to serve others in love. We create opportunities for students as well as families to experience community and grow in their roles. Students will learn to understand the importance of serving and loving others as disciples of Jesus Christ, all while having fun!
